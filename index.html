Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Mexican Fiesta Bites

    Like mini tostadas, thin pretzel crisps are piled high with refried beans, guacamole, salsa, sour cream, and more! Lutzflcat describes them as "creamy, one-bite hors d'oeuvres packed with fresh flavor."

  • Marvelous Mini Mexican Quiches

    Easy to make and always a hit, these Mexican mini-quiches are bursting with flavor. "This is truly amazing," says SamSmidgett. "Although they are so good on their own, I dipped them in a little hot sauce and it made them POP. Man alive they went quickly. Make a double batch!"

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Creamy Asparagus and Mushroom Pasta

    This creamy asparagus and mushroom pasta is a delicious meatless main dish for 4, or a fantastic side for 6, great to go with chicken or chops. A tossed green salad and some garlic bread go nicely with this.

  • Shrimp Biryani

    This shrimp biryani is aromatic and complex, homey and comforting. A mixture of spices, bloomed in ghee, flavor both the rice and tomato sauce, and the entire dish is baked layered with spiced yogurt-marinated shrimp.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Bougatsa

    Bougatsa, a Greek pastry that's delicious for breakfast or anytime, has a creamy but sturdy lemon-vanilla custard baked between layers of crisp and flaky phyllo dough.

  • Margarita Bars

    These margarita bars are perfectly sweet, and oh, so creamy. The filling is like a cross between Key lime pie and a lemon bar, with a hint of tequila.
